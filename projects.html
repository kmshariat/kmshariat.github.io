Research and Projects

  • Supervised Machine Learning on Dynamic Dataset Using 1D CNN for Speaker Recognition [2023]
    • Mentor: Dr. Md Rasedujjaman
    We used 1D CNN to recognize patterns in human speech audio files. The accuracy of our Model is 0.95. It can identify the speaker of the audio file. If it cannot it’ll ask for the name of the speaker and predict the name in the future.
  • Mass of the Central Black Hole of Active Galactic Nuclei: Reverberation Mapping [2023]
    • Team Lead: Ahmad Al-Imtiaz
    We use archive data to extract data from the spectra of an AGN. Using the spectral data, we calculate the mass of the agn. Find the technical report here.
  • Visualizing Fourier Series Using Op-Amp Adder Circuit [2022]
    • Mentor: Dr. Md Rasedujjaman
    We used 741 Op-Amp to create an adder circuit. Three inputs of the adder circuit are then connected to the three inputs of the signal generators working as the first three harmonics of the Fourier Series.
  • Polarity Detection Using LEDs and Their Usage [2022]
    • Mentor: Dr. Mohammad Kamruzzaman Khan Prince
    We used Light Emitting Diodes to detect the polarity of the input voltage source. The methodology is based on the triggering voltage characteristics of a diode.
