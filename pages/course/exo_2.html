of exoplanet detection can be put forward like this. We live in a solar system with eight planets, a star and many small rocky objects. But still we don't have a clear understanding of how our solar system formed. Studying exoplanet systems can help us solve this problem. We cannot stay in this planet forever. The Sun is now a main sequence star. After 7.6 billion years the radius of the Sun will become 1.2 astronomical unit engulfing the earth. If civilization wants to thrive in the future, we need to find alternative habitable home for humans. The most interesting thing is, it has the potential to solve the mystery of origin of life problem. If there are life on other planets, then we can study what's the similarity between our planet and their planet and what's the difference with other barren planets. For these reasons, exoplanet hunting has become one of the hot topics of astronomy.
